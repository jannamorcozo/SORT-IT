Create a PVC Cable and Wire Organizer

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) Plastic
Loose cables and wires can quickly turn into a tangled mess in drawers, bags, or workspaces. Instead of throwing away small PVC pipe scraps, you can turn them into a simple, durable, and reusable cable organizer. This DIY project is beginner-friendly, low-cost, and customizable—perfect for organizing chargers, earphones, USB cables, and small extension cords while keeping them protected and easy to identify.

Materials Needed

  • Short PVC pipe sections (5–10 cm long)
  • Sandpaper (medium grit and fine grit)
  • Marker or pencil
  • PVC cutter, hacksaw, or fine-tooth saw
  • Paint, stickers, or adhesive labels (optional)
  • Damp cloth

Steps:

  1. Prepare the PVC pipes. Collect small PVC pipe scraps and check that they are clean and dry. If the pipes are dirty, wash them with mild soap and water, then let them dry completely.
  2. Cut the pipes to size. Using a PVC cutter or hacksaw, cut the pipes into sections about 5–10 cm long. Shorter pieces are ideal for earphones and USB cables. Longer pieces work better for thicker charging cables or small extension cords.
  3. Smooth the edges. Sand both ends of each PVC piece using medium-grit sandpaper to remove rough edges. Finish with fine-grit sandpaper for a smooth, safe surface that won’t damage cables or scratch your hands.
  4. Clean off dust. Wipe each pipe section with a damp cloth to remove PVC dust from sanding. Allow them to dry before use or decoration.
  5. Decorate or label (optional). Paint the pipes in different colors to match your workspace or color-code cables. Use stickers or adhesive labels to identify what each tube contains (e.g., “Phone Charger,” “Earphones,” “USB-C”). Let paint or markers dry completely.
  6. Organize the cables. Coil each cable neatly and slide it into a PVC tube. The rigid pipe helps keep cables from bending sharply or tangling.
  7. Store or display. Place the organizers in a drawer, backpack, or storage box. You can also stack them vertically in a container for easy access on a desk or shelf.
