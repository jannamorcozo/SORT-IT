DIY Paint Palette

PP (Polypropylene) Plastic
Polypropylene (PP) plastic lids or trays can be repurposed as paint palettes or mixing surfaces. They are lightweight, durable, and easy to clean, making them ideal for art projects, school work, or craft activities.

Materials Needed

  • Flat PP lids or trays (clean and intact)
  • Soap and water for cleaning

Steps:

  1. Clean the plastic. Wash the PP lids or trays thoroughly with soap and water to remove any residue. Dry completely before use.
  2. Prepare the palette. Place the lid or tray on a flat surface. The smooth surface serves as a mixing area for paints, glue, or other craft materials.
  3. Use for mixing. Pour small amounts of paint or materials onto the tray and mix as needed. The raised edges of lids help contain the materials.
  4. Clean after use. After finishing your project, rinse the tray with water and mild soap. Wipe or air-dry completely.
  5. Reuse multiple times. Store the cleaned palette for future art or craft activities. PP plastic is durable and can last for many uses.
