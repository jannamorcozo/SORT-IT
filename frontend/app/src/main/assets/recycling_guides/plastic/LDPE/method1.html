Precise Plant Watering Tool

LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) Plastic
Squeeze bottles with long nozzles (often used for condiments or laboratory liquids) can be transformed into watering tools for plants. The long nozzle allows for precise watering, making it ideal for indoor plants, seedlings, or hard-to-reach areas. This method repurposes LDPE plastic containers, which are commonly used for their flexibility and durability.

Materials Needed

  • Clean long-nozzle squeeze bottle
  • Warm water and soap

Steps:

  1. Inspect the container. Check the LDPE bottle for cracks, holes, or damage. Only use containers that are structurally sound to ensure your watering tool is sturdy.
  2. Clean thoroughly. Wash the bottle inside and out with soap and water. Remove any labels if desired. Let it dry completely.
  3. Fill the bottle with water, leaving some space at the top to allow for squeezing.
  4. Use the bottle to water indoor plants, seedlings, or hard-to-reach pots by gently squeezing the bottle to control the flow.
