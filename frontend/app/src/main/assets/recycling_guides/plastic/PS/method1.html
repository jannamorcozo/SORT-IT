Make Decorative Crafts

PS (Polystyrene) Plastic
Polystyrene foam sheets and shapes are lightweight, easy to cut, and ideal for creating decorative home crafts. With simple tools and materials, you can transform them into frames, wall décor, ornaments, or personalized gifts.

Materials Needed

  • Clean polystyrene foam sheets or pre-shaped foam pieces
  • Scissors or craft knife
  • Paint, markers, or glue
  • Optional: stickers, glitter, or other embellishments

Steps:

  1. Select suitable foam. Choose foam sheets or pieces that are clean, unbroken, and free from dust or debris. Avoid cracked or brittle pieces.
  2. Plan your design. Decide on the shapes or patterns you want to create. Sketch lightly on the foam if necessary.
  3. Cut into desired shapes. Use scissors for simple cuts or a craft knife for intricate designs. Take care to cut slowly and safely.
  4. Decorate the foam. Paint, color with markers, or glue decorative items such as glitter or paper cutouts onto the foam. Let paint or glue dry completely.
  5. Assemble if needed. For frames or layered ornaments, glue multiple foam pieces together to create depth or patterns.
  6. Display your creations. Use the decorated foam for wall art, photo frames, hanging ornaments, or other decorative purposes around your home.
