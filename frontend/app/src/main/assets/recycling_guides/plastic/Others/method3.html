DIY Diskette Coasters

Other Plastics
Turn old floppy disks into unique, retro-inspired coasters. Using the magnetic disk from a diskettes and clear resin, you can create durable, heat-resistant coasters perfect for your drinks or as a fun gift.

Materials Needed

  • Old 3.5-inch floppy disks
  • Clear epoxy resin (heat-resistant)
  • Mixing cups and stir sticks
  • Sandpaper (optional, for smoothing edges)
  • Gloves and protective covering for workspace

Steps:

  1. Disassemble the diskette: Remove the plastic casing and metal shutter, leaving only the circular magnetic disk.
  2. Flatten the disk: Press gently if slightly warped; sand edges if needed.
  3. Prepare resin: Mix resin according to package instructions.
  4. Seal the disk: Pour resin over the magnetic disk in a mold or on a flat surface, spreading evenly.
  5. Cure: Let resin fully harden as per instructions (usually 24–48 hours).
  6. Finish: Remove from mold and smooth edges if necessary. Your coaster is ready to use!
