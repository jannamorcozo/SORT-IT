Paper Craft Projects

Old Newspapers
Give old newspaper a second life by turning it into a handmade paper basket. This project is great for organizing small items, decorating your desk, or creating a fun craft.

Materials Needed

  • Old/new paper (newspapers, office paper, colored paper)
  • Scissors, glue, tape
  • Paints, crayons, or markers
  • Ruler and pencil

Steps:

  1. Plan your craft project: decorations, boxes, or paper toys.
  2. Cut paper into desired shapes and sizes.
  3. Assemble pieces using glue or tape.
  4. Decorate with paints, markers, or other materials.
  5. Display or gift your handmade paper craft.
