Reuse Old/New Paper

Old Newspapers
Turn old newspapers into eco-friendly gift bags that are unique, creative, and completely recyclable. This DIY project is perfect for small presents and craft fun.

Materials Needed

  • Old newspapers, magazines, or scrap paper
  • Scissors, glue, tape
  • Markers, paint, or decorative items

Steps:

  1. Prepare the paper: Cut old newspapers or magazines into desired sizes and shapes.
  2. Create envelopes or gift wrap: Fold and glue paper to make simple gift bags or wrapping.
  3. Make origami figures or paper flowers: Use folded paper to create decorative items.
  4. Use scrap paper for notes, shopping lists, or practice sheets: Repurpose any leftover paper.
  5. Decorate notebooks or bind scrap paper into a DIY notebook: Create a personalized notebook from old scraps.
