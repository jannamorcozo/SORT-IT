Recycle Old/New Paper

Old Newspapers
Turn old newspapers into homemade recycled paper using simple items you can find at home.

Materials Needed

  • Old newspapers, notebooks, or office paper
  • Paper shredder or scissors (optional)
  • Clean water
  • Large bowl or bucket

Steps:

  1. Sort your paper by type and remove staples or plastic.
  2. Tear the paper into small pieces or shred it.
  3. Soak the paper in water for a few hours until it becomes pulp.
  4. Pour the pulp into a recycling bin or give it to your local recycling center.
  5. Let the recycled paper dry completely before reuse.
