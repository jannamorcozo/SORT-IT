Make a Small Notebook

Selected White Ledger Paper
Recycle your ledger sheets into a handy notebook or notepad for notes, sketches, or lists. It’s a creative and practical way to reuse paper.

Materials Needed

  • Clean white ledger sheets
  • Cardboard or thick paper for cover
  • Stapler, thread, or binder clip
  • Ruler and scissors

Steps:

  1. Select and stack sheets. Choose pages of similar size and smoothness for uniformity.
  2. Trim edges if needed. Use scissors or a paper cutter to make all pages even. This ensures a neat notebook.
  3. Prepare a cover. Cut a piece of cardboard slightly larger than the ledger sheets to protect the pages.
  4. Align pages and cover. Stack the sheets neatly and place the cardboard on top. Make sure the edges line up evenly.
  5. Bind the notebook. Staple along the spine, sew with thread, or use a binder clip to secure the pages together.
  6. Decorate the cover (optional). Add markers, stickers, or labels to personalize your notebook.
  7. Use your notebook. It’s ready for notes, sketches, journaling, or making lists.
