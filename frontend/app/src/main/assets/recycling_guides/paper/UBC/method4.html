Create a Pencil or Pen Holder

Used Beverage Cartons
Used beverage cartons can be repurposed into stylish and functional pencil or pen holders. This DIY project is eco-friendly, keeps your desk organized, and lets you customize the holder with your own designs.

Materials Needed

  • Beverage carton (clean and dry)
  • Scissors
  • Glue or tape (optional, for reinforcement or decoration)
  • Decorative paper, markers, or paint

Steps:

  1. Clean the carton. Rinse the inside of the beverage carton thoroughly to remove any leftover liquid. Let it dry completely to prevent mold or odors.
  2. Cut the top. Using scissors, cut the top portion of the carton to create an open container. Adjust the height depending on the length of your pens, pencils, or brushes (about 10–15 cm is usually ideal).
  3. Optional: Decorate the holder. Wrap the carton with decorative paper, paint, or draw designs with markers. You can also use stickers or washi tape to personalize it.
  4. Reinforce if needed. If the carton feels flimsy, you can tape the edges or add a layer of glue to make it sturdier.
  5. Fill with stationery. Place pens, pencils, markers, or paintbrushes inside the holder. Arrange neatly for easy access.
  6. Use on your desk. Keep the holder on your study table, office desk, or craft area. It’s a simple way to organize stationery while recycling.
