How to Make a Wire Heart Sculpture

Copper
Create a charming, handmade heart sculpture using simple copper wire. This easy craft is perfect as a table decoration, a thoughtful gift ornament, or even a wall hanger. With just a few tools and some creativity, you can make a delicate heart shape that adds a personal touch to any space.

Materials Needed

  • 1–2 meters of copper wire (18–20 gauge works well for bending but is sturdy enough to hold shape)
  • Pliers (needle-nose recommended for precise bends)
  • Wire cutters
  • Optional: Ribbon or beads for decoration

Steps:

  1. Prepare the wire. Cut a 1-meter piece of copper wire using wire cutters. If you want a larger heart, cut up to 2 meters.
  2. Find the midpoint. Fold the wire gently in half to locate the center. This midpoint will form the bottom tip of your heart.
  3. Shape the curves. Hold each half of the wire and carefully bend it outward to form symmetrical curves for the heart’s top lobes. Use your hands for larger curves, or pliers for precise shaping. Adjust until both sides look even and heart-shaped.
  4. Secure the bottom. Twist the wire ends together at the bottom point of the heart to keep the shape intact. Make sure the twist is tight enough so the heart doesn’t unravel.
  5. Optional: Add a stand. To make the heart stand upright, cut a small piece of wire and form a loop or spiral at the base. Attach it to the bottom twist to act as a stable stand.
  6. Optional: Decorate. You can wrap ribbon around the wire or add beads along the curves for extra flair.
