Traditional Recycling of Aluminum Cans

Aluminum Tin
Aluminum is highly recyclable and can be reused indefinitely without losing quality. Returning clean aluminum items to recycling streams reduces landfill waste, conserves energy, and supports the circular economy. This method is simple, safe, and the most environmentally responsible option for aluminum products.

Materials Needed

  • Aluminum cans, trays, foil, or lids
  • Water for rinsing

Steps:

  1. Rinse the aluminum. Wash cans, trays, or foil to remove any food residue. Ensure they are clean to avoid contamination in recycling.
  2. Flatten cans (optional). Press or crush aluminum cans to save space in bins or during transport.
  3. Sort and recycle. Place clean aluminum items in designated recycling bins. Alternatively, bring to a scrap dealer or recycling center that accepts aluminum.
