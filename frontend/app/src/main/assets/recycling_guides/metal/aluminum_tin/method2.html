How to Make a Candle Holders and Lanterns

Aluminum Tin
Used aluminum cans can be transformed into simple candle holders or lanterns that add a warm, decorative glow to outdoor spaces or table settings. This is an easy upcycling project that gives single-use cans a second life while creating functional décor.

Materials Needed

  • Empty aluminum can
  • Mild soap and water
  • Towel or cloth
  • Nail, awl, or small screwdriver
  • Hammer (optional)
  • Marker or pen
  • Tea light candle or LED candle
  • Heat-resistant surface

Steps:

  1. Clean the can. Wash the can with mild soap and water. Remove any labels and adhesive. Dry completely with a towel.
  2. Prepare the design. Use a marker to draw dots or patterns on the can. Simple shapes or evenly spaced dots work best.
  3. Punch decorative holes. Place the can on a stable surface. Carefully punch holes along the marked pattern using a nail or awl. Tap gently with a hammer if needed.
  4. Smooth sharp edges. Check the top rim and holes for sharp edges. Gently press or sand rough areas to reduce cuts.
  5. Insert the candle. Place a tea light or LED candle inside the can. Ensure it sits flat at the bottom.
  6. Display and use. Set the lantern on a heat-resistant surface. Use outdoors or as table décor for soft ambient lighting.
