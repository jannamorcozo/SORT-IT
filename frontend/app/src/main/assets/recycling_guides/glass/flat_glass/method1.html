Recycling Flat Glass at a Collection Facility

Flat Glass
Flat glass such as window panes, mirrors, and glass tabletops should be taken to specialized recycling centers because they are processed differently from bottles and jars.

Materials Needed

  • Flat glass (window glass, mirrors, tabletops)
  • Cardboard or old newspaper
  • Gloves

Steps:

  1. Inspect the glass for cracks or sharp edges before handling.
  2. Wear gloves to protect your hands from cuts.
  3. Wrap the glass securely with cardboard or several layers of newspaper.
  4. Tape the wrapping to prevent the glass from shifting.
  5. Transport the glass carefully to a designated glass recycling facility.
  6. Inform staff that you are disposing of flat glass and follow their instructions.
