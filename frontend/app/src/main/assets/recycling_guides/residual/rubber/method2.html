Garden Stepping Pads

Rubber
Old rubber mats, or sturdy plastic can be repurposed into garden stepping pads. These pads help create safe walkways, protect soil, and add a creative touch to your garden while reusing materials.

Materials Needed

  • Old rubber mats, or sturdy plastic
  • Scissors, utility knife, or saw (depending on material)
  • Paint (optional, for decoration)
  • Sandpaper or wire brush (for smoothing edges)
  • Soil or gravel (optional, for embedding)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the materials. Clean tires, mats, or plastic. Remove dirt and smooth rough edges.
  2. Cut to shape (optional). Trim pads into desired shapes and even edges.
  3. Decorate (optional). Paint with weather-resistant paint and let dry.
  4. Place stepping pads. Arrange along your garden path and press into soil or gravel for stability.
  5. Test and adjust. Step on each pad to ensure stability. Adjust positions as needed.
