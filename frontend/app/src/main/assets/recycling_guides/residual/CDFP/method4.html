Straw Bracelets Out of Plastic Straws

Clean and Dry Flexible Plastics
Colorful plastic or paper straws can be repurposed into fun and lightweight bracelets. This craft is easy, eco-friendly, and perfect for kids, beginners, or creative DIY projects.

Materials Needed

  • Clean, colorful plastic or paper straws
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread (or elastic cord)
  • Flattening tool (optional: rolling pin or spoon)
  • Ruler (optional, for consistent bead size)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the straws. Cut the straws into small, uniform sections (about 1–2 cm each) to make beads. Optional: Flatten the straw pieces slightly with a rolling pin or spoon to make them easier to thread.
  2. Thread the beads. Thread the straw pieces onto a needle and strong thread or elastic cord. Alternate colors or patterns for a creative design. Continue until the thread reaches the desired bracelet length.
  3. Secure the bracelet. Tie the ends of the thread or elastic cord securely in a knot. Trim any excess thread or cord.
  4. Finish and wear. Check that all beads are snug and the knot is secure. Wear your bracelet or gift it as a handmade accessory.
